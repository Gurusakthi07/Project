World Cup 2023

The World Cup 2023 was conducted between the ten Countries.The Countries are INDIA,AUSTRALIA,SOUTH AFRICA,NEWZEALAND,PAKISTAN,BANGALADESH,AFGHANISTAN,SRILANKA,NETHARLANDS,ENGLAND.These teams have been selected on the basis of the ICC rankings.The Rankings has been given by the results of their tour to the other country.World Cup 2023 was held in INDIA.Each team can compete with other nine teams.The teams in top 4 in the points table has been qualified to the semi finals.Then Winning Teams of the Semi Finals has been play the finals.The price amount for the winner is 33crore.The price amount for the runner is 16crore.The venues of WC 2023 are Chennai, Mumbai,Bangaluru,Kolkata,Dharamshala,Delhi,Ahamadabad,Lucknow.If semifinals are affected by the rain then the reserverd day are allocated.
